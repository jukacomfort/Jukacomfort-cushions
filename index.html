The Solution

  • ✔ Enhances comfort during long sessions
  • ✔ Maintains proper alignment and stability
  • ✔ Easy to use, remove, and clean

How It Works

  1. Slip the cushions over your reformer’s shoulder blocks
  2. Begin your workout as usual
  3. Enjoy greater comfort and freedom of movement
