Our Mission

At Juka Comfort, we strive to enhance your Pilates experience with our specially designed shoulder block cushions. We believe that comfort and support are essential for achieving optimal performance during your workouts.

Made from soft, durable materials, they’re built to support you through every session and every level of your practice. And when the need arises, just toss them in the wash — they’re as low maintenance as they are comfortable.

Our Story

We started JuKa Comfort out of necessity. The reformer shoulder blocks hurt my shoulders and kept me from focusing on my flow and core. Even though the shoulder blocks on the reformer are padded, they are tough on shoulders when using heavy or even light springs! Our shoulder block cushions are soft, durable, and functional. They slip on easily and improve comfort — increasing the value of your workout.
